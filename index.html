/ 05 · Direct Answers

The things people actually ask.

What does this actually cost? +
The audit is always free. Entry builds start from £497 — typically a high-converting front-end or a starter lead system. Above that, we offer two larger one-off builds (full sites and multi-system installs from £1,497) and two monthly retainer tiers from £497/mo. Every engagement is scoped to what actually moves your numbers — no packaged upsells. We guarantee every build pays for itself or we keep working until it does.
How is this different from a marketing agency? +
Agencies sell you activity — ads, posts, campaigns. We sell you infrastructure that replaces activity. One system runs for years. One campaign runs for a month. We build the thing agencies sell you as a service.
Do I need existing tools or tech stack? +
No. Most clients come to us with a phone, an email, and a notes app. We bring the stack — and we only install what's needed. No bloat, no 14-tool subscriptions, no learning curve for your team.
Will AI be answering my customers? +
Only where it should. AI drafts, routes, scores, and captures — you approve and close. Every system is built so the human stays in the loop on anything that matters. No one ever gets a "chatbot experience" when they shouldn't.
How long before I see results? +
Missed-call recovery typically shows recovered revenue in week one. Lead pipelines and websites usually take 3–4 weeks to show real lift as the data accumulates. If we don't see measurable lift in 60 days, we keep working for free until you do.
Who isn't this for? +
Pre-revenue founders, enterprises with in-house engineering, anyone negotiating price before they've seen the audit, and anyone who thinks "marketing plan" when they mean "system." If that's you — we'll save you time and decline the call.
